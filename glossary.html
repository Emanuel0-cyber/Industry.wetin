No Grammar Glossary

Big words explained like you’re 5.

KPI

Full meaning: Key Performance Indicator

Wetin be this: Number that shows if you’re doing well. Like exam score for business.

ROI

Full meaning: Return On Investment

Wetin be this: If you put ₦10k inside business, how much extra comes back?

Stakeholder

Wetin be this: Anybody wey your work go affect. Your boss, customer, even government.

B2B

Full meaning: Business to Business

Wetin be this: Company selling to another company. Not to regular people.
