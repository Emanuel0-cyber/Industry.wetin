Skills That Work Everywhere

Learn these once, use them in any industry.

Excel / Google Sheets

What: Tool for organizing numbers and data.

Why: Every company uses it. From banks to farms.

Start: YouTube: "Excel for Beginners" by Kevin Stratvert

Communication

What: Explaining your ideas clearly.

Why: Best engineers still need to talk to people.

Start: Practice summarizing movies to friends in 30 seconds.

Problem Solving

What: Breaking big wahala into small steps.

Why: All jobs are really just solving problems.

Start: Play chess or sudoku 10 mins/day.
